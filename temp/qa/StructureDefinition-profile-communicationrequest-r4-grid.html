NameCard.TypeConstraints and Usage
  CommunicationRequest 0..*dom-2: If the resource is contained in another resource, it SHALL NOT contain nested Resources
dom-4: If a resource is contained in another resource, it SHALL NOT have a meta.versionId or a meta.lastUpdated
dom-3: If the resource is contained in another resource, it SHALL be referred to from elsewhere in the resource or SHALL refer to the containing resource
dom-6: A resource should have narrative for robust management
dom-5: If a resource is contained in another resource, it SHALL NOT have a security label
Definition:
A request to convey information; e.g. the CDS system proposes that an alert be sent to a responsible provider, the CDS system proposes that the public health agency be notified about a reportable condition.

  identifier 0..*IdentifierDefinition:
Business identifiers assigned to this communication request by the performer or other systems which remain constant as the resource is updated and propagates from server to server.


Comments:
This is a business identifier, not a resource identifier (see discussion). It is best practice for the identifier to only appear on a single resource instance, however business practices may occasionally dictate that multiple resource instances with the same identifier can exist - possibly even with different resource types. For example, multiple Patient and a Person resource instance might share the same social insurance number.

  basedOn 1..1Reference(Resource)Definition:
A plan or proposal that is fulfilled in whole or in part by this request.

  status 1..1codeBinding: RequestStatus (required)
Fixed Value: draft
Definition:
The status of the proposal or order.

  subject 1..1Reference(CRD Patient)Definition:
The patient or group that is the focus of this communication request.

  occurrence[x] 0..1dateTime, PeriodDefinition:
The time when this communication is to occur.

  authoredOn 1..1dateTimeDefinition:
For draft requests, indicates the date of initial creation. For requests with other statuses, indicates the date of activation.

  requester 1..1Reference(CRD Practitioner | CRD PractitionerRole)Definition:
The device, individual, or organization who initiated the request and has responsibility for its activation.

  recipient 0..*Reference(CRD Practitioner | CRD PractitionerRole)Definition:
The entity (e.g. person, organization, clinical information system, device, group, or care team) which is the intended target of the communication.

  sender 0..1Reference(CRD Practitioner | CRD PractitionerRole)Definition:
The entity (e.g. person, organization, clinical information system, or device) which is to be the source of the communication.

  reasonCode 0..*CodeableConceptBinding: v3 Code System ActReason (example)
Definition:
Describes why the request is being made in coded or textual form.


Comments:
Textual reasons can be captured using reasonCode.text.
